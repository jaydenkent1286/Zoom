If I Were President — Zoom Discussion

A friendly online meetup to brainstorm and share what you'd do if you were president. All ideas welcome — policy, culture, climate, education, and more.

About the session

This session is an open discussion where participants share policies they'd introduce, priorities they'd set, and questions they'd ask a president. It's intended for respectful conversation and idea-sharing.

Agenda

  • Introductions (5–10 min)
  • Lightning policy pitches (20–30 min)
  • Open debate / follow-ups (20 min)
  • Q&A & next steps (10 min)
